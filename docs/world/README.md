# Math Learning World — Creative Foundation

> **A world where mathematics is alive.**

This directory is the creative Source of Truth for the Math Learning World universe. It governs the meaning of the world before implementation details, while remaining subordinate to verified product and technical constraints where those constraints are explicit.

## Authority order

1. Universe Bible — why the world exists and what its laws mean.
2. Learning Philosophy — how wonder becomes durable understanding.
3. World Bible — geography, regions, routes, and emotional progression.
4. Civilization Bible — how people live with mathematics.
5. Landmark Bible — production targets for major places.
6. Visual Language — composition, materials, light, color, and readability.
7. Design System — reusable decision rules across art, gameplay, UI, audio, and learning.

## Foundational statement

Math Learning World is not a lesson wrapper and not a quiz game with scenery. It is a living world in which curiosity, construction, exploration, and reflection allow mathematical ideas to become visible and useful.

## Highest creative principle

**First Wonder, Then Knowledge.**

Before the player is asked to explain, the world should give them something worth noticing. Before a formula is named, the player should experience the pattern or relationship that makes the formula meaningful.

## Seven pillars

1. Wonder First.
2. Understanding Over Memorization.
3. Play Creates Meaning.
4. Nature Reveals Mathematics.
5. Every Place Teaches.
6. Progress Through Mastery.
7. Beauty Supports Learning.

## Decision gate

Every major feature, place, quest, interaction, and presentation layer should answer five questions:

- Does it create curiosity?
- Does it invite experimentation?
- Can the player discover something through action?
- Does it deepen understanding of the world?
- Is it still meaningful without points or rewards?

A weak answer is not an automatic rejection, but it is a signal that the design needs a clearer purpose.
