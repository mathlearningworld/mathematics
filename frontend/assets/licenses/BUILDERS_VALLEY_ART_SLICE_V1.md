# Builder's Valley Art Vertical Slice v1 — Asset Provenance

All visual assets listed in `builders-valley-art-slice-v1.json` are original,
code-generated project assets created specifically for Math Learning World.

- No Shutterstock preview, marketplace image, copied sprite sheet, or external
  game artwork is included.
- The assets are implemented as reusable Phaser primitives in
  `frontend/src/sandbox/art/pixelArtFactory.js`.
- Semantic asset IDs are stable even when their visual implementation is
  replaced by future production sprite sheets.
- Any future third-party asset must be added to the manifest with its source,
  creator, license, acquisition date, and permitted product use before it may
  enter the game repository.

This record exists to preserve provenance and prevent accidental use of
search-result imagery as production material.
